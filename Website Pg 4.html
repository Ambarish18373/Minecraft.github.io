1) Dream:



Dream is a YouTuber and Twitch streamer known primarily for Minecraft content. He began his YouTube career in 2014 and gained substantial popularity in 2019 and 2020 having uploaded videos based around the game Minecraft. He is well known for his YouTube series Minecraft Manhunt, and for speedrunning Minecraft. Content created in his Dream SMP Minecraft server has also attracted considerable attention. As of June 2021, his seven YouTube channels have collectively reached over 33.8 million subscribers and over 2.36 billion views. YouTube awarded Dream the Streamy Award for Gaming in 2020.

2) Technoblade:



Technoblade (also known as Techno, The Blade, or The Blood God) is an American gaming YouTuber known primarily for Minecraft-centered content. He created his YouTube account in 2013 and gained substantial popularity in 2020 from the $100,000 Dream vs Technoblade Duel. He is commonly regarded in the Minecraft Community as one of the best PvP players. As of May 2021, his main YouTube channel has reached over 7.24 million subscribers and over 836 million views. In 2020 Technoblade became a member of the DreamSMP.

3) Illumina:


Illumina is a very talented Minecraft speedrunner, and has the accolades on the leaderboards to prove it. He has a multitude of high rankings on the speedrunning Minecraft leaderboard for Java Edition.Illumina boasts seven top 10 rankings for different Minecraft Java Edition speedrunning categories, with two being first place.He still even holds the Minecraft Java Any% Random Seed for Pre 1.9 record that he set back in 2018.

There is no video On Illumina but you can always check his speedruns on his channel.
