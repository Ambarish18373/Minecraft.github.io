This Is The Main Boss: The Ender Dragon



Ways of playing the Game

First way:
The First Way is also called Speed run. When you first spawn in the world your first aim will be to get wood. Wood is the most essential item in the game. After that craft wooden tools like a Pickaxe and an Axe. Dig 4-5 blocks down and mine stone. With that being done get your crafting table and craft stone tools. Next fetch for Animals as your saturation bar might be low. You can cook the food in the furnace using Coal or Wood. I would prefer you to cook the food than eating it raw because cooked food fills more saturation points. If you are speedrunning for a record then you will need atleast 3 iron ingots to craft a bucket and then you can fill water in it. This is needed to build the nether portal: The gateway to the Nether world. You will also need 10 sources of lava. The tutorial to build it will be available on Youtube. Light up the portal with a 'Flint and Steel' and enter the portal. After entering the Nether you should look for a Nether fortress. After finding the fortress find a blaze spawner and kill blazes untill you get 7-8 blaze rods. Then go and mine gold to trade with piglins. Piglins are hostile mobs which trade ender pearls for gold ingots. After that craft your blaze rods into blaze powder and combine it with the pearls to craft eye of ender. Exit the nether and proceed towards the stronghold. You can do this by throwing your ender eyes. After finding the stronghold find the portal room and fill the eyes in the portal frames. Then enter the End Dimension and kill the dragon Using the Bed method or your Sword/Axe. Then you have finally beaten the game.

Second Way:
The first way is to start collecting materials for a base. You can build a base, gather resources useful for the dragon fight like:
1) Get netherite armor(the best armor in the game).
2) Enchant all weaponry and armor.
3) Collect 12 ender pearls (minimum needed to fill the end portal).
4) Get 6 blaze rods (minimum needed to craft the ender eyes).
5) Locate the End portal.
6) Kill the ender dragon.

You can also build a lot of automatic farms, villager trading halls etc. You can also build map arts.

Different Automatic Farms:
1) Iron Farm

This farm Can be useful to get tons of iron for crafting items


2) Gold Farm

This farm can be useful for crafting food items like golden apples and golden carrots which is the best food in the game.


3) All Mob Farm

This farm can be useful for getting bones for taming dogs. It also gets you rotten flesh to feed your dogs.

4) Gunpowder Farm

This farm can be useful to get gunpowder to craft rockets. Rockets can get you infinite flight by using Elytra.

5) Enderman Xp Farm

This farm can be useful for getting xp. You can use this xp to enchant and repair your weaponry.
